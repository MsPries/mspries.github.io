Hockey Predictor
Hockey Predictor takes the halftime commentary of NHL Hockey games and predicts the winner. It has been correct for 83% of the games in the 2018 season.
Data Source
We found our training data on the NHL website, where it is publicly available. We used halftime commentary from each season 2004-2017. The halftime commentary from several games was cut short either due to breaking news or technical error, so these commentaries were omitted. Our training set consists of 5,600 commentaries.
Data Cleaning
We cleaned our data by removing advertisements and words unrecognized by the NLTK dictionary, except for proper nouns. We replaced proper nouns with PLAYER for any player name and team names with TEAM1, TEAM2, or TEAM_OTHER to align the teams being described with the teams competing or a team being described that is not currently competing.
We detected technical glitches by setting a minimum commentary of 500 words. We omitted any commentary that did not contain 500 words.
